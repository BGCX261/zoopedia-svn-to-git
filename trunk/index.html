January 28--Kayaking Sungei Khatib Bongsu

Explore Sungei Khatib Bongsu by kayak, in a 7.5 km relaxing mangrove paddle amongst one of the last accessible riverine mangroves on mainland Singapore. Expect to see a variety of interesting plant and animal life. Suitable for beginners and for those who have never kayaked before. Bring sun protection, water, snacks, and a change of clothes (will get wet!). Toilets with showers are available. Up to 3 children can be accommodated on this trip, and must be accompanied by one parent per child.
