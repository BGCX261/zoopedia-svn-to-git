Fuzzy Catterpillar

What You Need:

•   8 - 1-inch sparkly-tinsel pom-poms
•   2 - small googly eyes (or a couple of 3mm black pom-poms)
•   A 4-inch piece of recycled telephone wire or thin floral wire
•   White glue

What to do:

Use glue to connect one pom-pom to the next in a straight line. After all 8 pom-poms are connected, let them dry for about 20 minutes.

Glue eyes on the front of the caterpillar.

Place the wire underneath and between the first and second pom-pom.

Bring it up over the "head" to form antennae. If you want to, bend the tips of the wire into small balls like you see on some caterpillars' antennae.
