Animal of the Day

Tiger
Area:Scattered, Small Areas in Asia and Africa


Habitat:Tropical Rain Forest,Snow covered Area, Rain Forest swamps ,Drier forest areas


Food :Pigs ,Deer and other herbivorous animals


Size: Full grown males are up 6 to 8 feet long


Babies: An average litter is about 2 to 3 cubs weighing about 2 pounds each
